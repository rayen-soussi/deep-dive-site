A few common questions

Is Deep Dive free?

Yes. The core app is free to use, in full, for as long as you'd like.

Do I need an account?

No. Deep Dive works completely on its own. An account is only there if you'd like your space to follow you across devices.

Does it work offline?

Yes, fully. Your tasks, courses, and notes live on your device, whether or not you're connected.

What if I find a problem, or have an idea?

Send it our way at the email above. Small notes are just as welcome as bigger ones.
